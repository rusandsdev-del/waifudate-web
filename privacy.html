Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Waifu Date Simulation app (Android), developed by rusandsdev. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Username: Stored locally on your device only.
  • Chat messages: Sent to Groq API to generate AI responses. Not stored on our servers.
  • App usage data: Daily message count, love level, energy, and progress stored locally on your device.

2. AI-Generated Content

Chat responses are generated by an AI language model (Groq API). Responses may occasionally be inaccurate. "Kazuha" is a fictional character, not a real person.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — displays rewarded ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Groq API — processes chat messages. Groq Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — handles in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 17 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Advertising

This app shows rewarded ads via Google AdMob. You can opt out via Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
